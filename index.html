About The bytexl

Discover our passion for culinary excellence

Our Journey

Founded in 2012, The bytexl began as a dream to create a dining experience that combines traditional flavors with modern culinary techniques. Our founder, Chef Marcus Rodriguez, started this journey with a simple vision: to serve exceptional food in an atmosphere that feels like home.

Over the years, we've grown from a small neighborhood bistro to a renowned dining destination, but our core values remain the same. We believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients and treating every guest like family.

Today, The bytexl stands as a testament to what passion, dedication, and exceptional service can achieve. We're not just a restaurant; we're a place where memories are made, celebrations happen, and communities come together.

Our Journey

Founded in 2012, The bytexl began as a dream to create a dining experience that combines traditional flavors with modern culinary techniques. Our founder, Chef Marcus Rodriguez, started this journey with a simple vision: to serve exceptional food in an atmosphere that feels like home.

Over the years, we've grown from a small neighborhood bistro to a renowned dining destination, but our core values remain the same. We believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients and treating every guest like family.

Today, The Urban Fork stands as a testament to what passion, dedication, and exceptional service can achieve. We're not just a restaurant; we're a place where memories are made, celebrations happen, and communities come together.
